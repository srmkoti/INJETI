International Journals on Emerging Technologies and innovations

The primary objective of this is to bring together academicians, researchers, industrialists, scientists, entrepreneurs, faculty and students to share their knowledge in cutting edge technologies, innovations and recent trends in the field of computational Intelligence and Data Analytics. The Journal provides a platform to showcase their research interest in the respective domains. We are inviting all scientists, academicians, business delegates and students from all over the world to attend the International Journals on Emerging Technologies and innovations(INJETI).

TOPICS

INJETI invites authors to contribute to the conference by submitting original, unpublished journals that illustrate Research results, Research Projects, Methodologies, Algorithms, surveys/Reviews, Case studies, and Industrial experiences that describes significant advances in the field of Computer Science and Technologies.

  • Fuzzy Systems
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Machine Learning
  • Evolutionary Computing
  • Intelligent Control
  • Image Processing
  • Computer Graphics

The authors of INCETI 2023 should pay attention to the following:

1. Original, previously unpublished papers, and not simultaneously submitted to another journal or conference, with scientific and technical contributions, are expected.

2. All submitted papers will be checked for plagiarism through the IEEE CrossCheck system or Turnitin software.

3. Papers must be written in English with a maximum paper length of six (6) printed pages including figures for regular papers. If they exceed 6 pages, author must pay extra fee 50 Euro for per paper.

4. Papers have to be formatted in the conference proceedings layout on A4 paper according to IEEE Template as follows: WORD: Microsoft Word Template LATEX: LaTeXTemplate

5. Authors of accepted papers should upload their final paper in PDF format. The PDF file must be compliance with IEEE eXpress PDF format.

6. Only accepted and presented papers will be published in the INCETI 2023 Conference Proceedings which will be submitted to the IEEE Xplore® digital library
